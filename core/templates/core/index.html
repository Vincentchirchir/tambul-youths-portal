Our Core Programs

Empowering Members Through Action

Tambul Hustle Youth Group brings together young people with a shared mission of building a bright future.
The group empowers members through collective savings, small loans, and welfare support.
Members contribute monthly, access affordable loans of Ksh 1,000–3,000 at 10% interest.
Members stand together through welfare initiatives - driven by unity.

Microloan & Savings Program

Affordable short-term loans and regular savings contributions that promote growth, responsibility, and financial inclusion among youth members.

Welfare & Support Fund

A solidarity system where members support one another during times of hardship such as illness, bereavement, or emergencies.

Community Projects Initiative

Collaborative ventures that empower members through skills, group farming, or small business projects that create shared income streams.

Transparency & Growth System

Digital record-keeping and reporting through our portal, ensuring clear accountability and confidence among members.
